OUTREACH
============
Ilakya Palanisamy, Joy Gu, Kavya Madhavan, Sarah Spivack, and Angela Lin (swe.berkeley.outreach@gmail.com)
Inspire and be inspired! As a member of SWE Outreach you will have the pleasure of working with enthusiastic young minds from the Berkeley area on exciting engineering based activities. Events such as SWE Science aim to spark children's interests in science, technology, engineering, and math (STEM). Our events are opportunities for primary and secondary school students to explore the thrills and challenges of engineering; and to be honorary engineers for a day! In addition, Outreach events are a great opportunity for you to take on mentorship and leadership roles. We are always looking for clever new ideas and projects to put on, and we need your help to lead the events. Get involved with Outreach to pass along your passion for STEM to bright, excited young minds!


OHP
===========
Joy Gu (ucb.swe.admit@gmail.com)
The Overnight Host Program is one of the most exciting events in the spring; partnering with ESS (Engineering Student Services) we invite new admits to stay overnight during Cal Day weekend to gain a first-hand view of what Berkeley can offer. We all remember college-decision time...and every one of you chose Berkeley! Join OHP and help newly admitted female engineers make their decisions by providing them with an amazing experience they can't forget! EVERYONE is welcome to apply!

Current Committee Members: Kavya Madhavan, Madelynn Whittaker, Huda Iftekhar, Ashlee Heuston, Sarah Chin, Rachael Boyle, Sarah Goldberg, Abirami Murugappan, Christine Choi, Alicia Auduong, Hana McLaughlin, Maria Gasch, Mindy Huang


PUBLIC RELATIONS
============
Michelle Yang and Sophia Liu (ucb.swe.pr@gmail.com)
The Public Relations committee is in charge of keeping SWE members in the loop about SWE events and opportunities. The main ways in which we do this are through preparing and distributing the weekly SWEmail, producing the newsletter that is distributed at general meetings, flyering around campus, chalking messages in classrooms, and posting social media updates including Facebook and Twitter. If you have a passion for writing, photography, design, communications/social media, or just an interest in public relations then we would love to have you join our team! (No experience necessary)
