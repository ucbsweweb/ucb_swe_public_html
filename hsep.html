High School Engineering Program


Comprised of high schoolers and a group of dedicated mentors, we aspire to help foster a nurturing environment for high school females to learn and explore their interests in engineering. Our 10-week program takes place from 9am to 12pm every Saturday and occurs in the fall (September through November) as well as the spring (February through May) every year. There is no cost, as SWE provides everything.
