SWENext


SWENext aims to encourage high school females to explore STEM and consider a STEM career by helping them establish SWENext clubs at their high schools. We provide students with resources to help them navigate high school, college, and career planning. If you are interested in starting a SWENext club, please refer to this document and reach out directly to the officers!

SWENext will also be hosting a one-day in-person SWENext Day that invites all the students from the affiliated clubs onto campus to tour Berkeley, attend panels from engineering professors about their own academic and career journey, learn how to 3D model and solder, and converse with current UC Berkeley SWE students about engineering and college life. We will also host a leadership summit as part of the day, helping students with improving their leadership skills. (Date: TBD)



In Fall 2024 - Spring 2025 year, SWENext will host:
OH Panel #1: Exploring Engineering Majors
OH Panel #2: Demystifying College Applications
OH Panel #3: Day in the Life of a Berkeley Engineer
