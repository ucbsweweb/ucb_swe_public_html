Engineering Day


Engineering Day is a free exciting Halloween themed-outreach event where K–3rd graders of all genders (and their parents/guardians) have the opportunity to participate and learn about STEM as we guide them through hands-on science experiments, talk to student engineers, participate in lighting talks with showcase, and have fun with some arts and crafts. We encourage everyone to dress up in costumes, as there will be a photo booth!

We invite all interested parents/guardians and teachers to sign up for our Elementary & Middle School Outreach Mailing List to get notified regarding registration for this and future Engineering Day events!


If you have any questions about this event, please contact us by email at swe.engineering.day@gmail.com.
