Engineering Day is a fun outreach event in October for K-3rd graders (and parents/guardians!) to interact with exciting, hands-on Halloween-themed science activities! We encourage everyone to dress up in costumes, as we will also be hosting a virtual costume parade for the kids!

We invite all interested parents/guardians and teachers to sign up for our Elementary & Middle School Outreach Mailing List to get notified regarding registration for this and future Engineering Day events!
